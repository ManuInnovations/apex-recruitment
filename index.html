Apex Recruitment is a recruitment service for the civil and construction industry. We offer a personable service to our clients and candidates. Our major focus is on Health and Safety, which we implement across our company and the construction sites we deal with. We cover Demolition, Residential, Commercial and Civil construction. Apex is currently based on the North Shore and covers all of Auckland city. We are 100% New Zealand owned and operated.
